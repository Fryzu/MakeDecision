Make decision project

Make decision is a place where users get simple answers from others in short closed questions like "what should I choose to wear today?".

This app is a Django 2.1 learning project with Bootstrap 4 frontend.

Features

  • users accounts
  • post management
  • *comments and feedback
  • *hashtags and searching
